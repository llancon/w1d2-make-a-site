The Chicago bears

Looking like cubs lately

Last game score was Bears 17- Cowboys 31

Hope they comeback strong

With a dismal start the Bears have fallen down to 0-3 to begin this season. Jay Cutler is out of the picture for now so the Bears will have to find a new stategy to make a comeback.

Devin Harris can't carry the team with punt returns and it looks like the defense has a a lot of kinks to work out for now.

Bears will have to rally for their next game. They will be facing the Lions at home, who have also lost their last game vesus the Greenbay Packers.

Next game will be have alot at stake because they are playing a same division team. A win would put them even for last place with the Lions.
